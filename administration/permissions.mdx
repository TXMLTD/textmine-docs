---
title: "Permissions and feature access"
description: "Control access to newer TextMine surfaces."
---

TextMine exposes newer product areas through feature flags, guards, and permissions.

## Key permissions

| Area | Permissions |
| --- | --- |
| Workflows | workflow:read, workflow:create |
| Playbooks | playbook:read, playbook:create |
| Records | records:read, records:write, records:delete |
| Vault | Vault access is controlled by Vault/team permissions and business scope. |
| Workbench | Workbench access is guarded separately and may require feature access. |

## Admin guidance

Only grant create/write permissions to users who should change reusable configuration or durable business data. Running agents, workflows, playbooks, imports, record updates, and document reprocessing can have broad operational effects.
