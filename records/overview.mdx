Records turn document evidence into structured business data.

![Records screenshot](/images/intercom/product-updates/26-may-2026/07-screenshot-2026-05-26-at-14-24-10.png)

Use Records when you need durable entities such as companies, agreements, people, vendors, properties, obligations, or custom business objects.

## Core concepts

- A schema defines record types, fields, and relationships.
- Candidate facts come from Vault documents and extracted tags.
- Review queues help confirm suggested changes before they update records.
- Evidence links keep records traceable back to source documents.
- The graph view shows relationships between records.
- Ask Records answers questions over structured records and supporting evidence.
