## Schemas

A record schema defines what records exist, which fields they contain, and how records can relate to one another. Schemas can be created or updated when the business needs a new structured data model.

## Candidates

TextMine can scan Vault tags and documents to suggest records, field values, and changes. Review candidates before accepting them so records stay trustworthy.

## Evidence

Each record field should remain connected to the documents and extracted evidence that support it. Use evidence links and audit events to understand where a value came from and when it changed.
