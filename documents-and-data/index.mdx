Use this section when you need to import, organize, extract, validate, publish, report on, or structure document-backed information in TextMine.

Start with Vault when the work begins with documents. Use Records when the output should become structured business data. Use Legislate for validated team-document views, Scribe for smart templates, and Reports and exports when you need shareable outputs.
