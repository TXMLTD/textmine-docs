Scribe is the template surface for TextMine. Use it to import Word templates, define fields, add conditional logic, and publish templates to teams.

## What Scribe is for

- Creating reusable templates.
- Adding fields and field groups.
- Adding conditional logic.
- Importing Word templates.
- Publishing templates to the right teams.
- Filling templates from Workbench or Workflows where configured.

## Modern template filling

Template filling can now be part of broader automation. Workbench can infer fill values from uploaded templates and context. Workflows can fill spreadsheet or form templates and use generated files in later steps, reports, or emails.
