We have added the following permission:

-

Allow users to create a personal team

We have made copy updates

-

"Create default tag" is now: "Manually insert a tag in the plain text"

-

"Edit default settings" is now: "Edit the default settings of a Vault Business Unit"

We have also rename the Settings section under Vault to "Custom Types" to avoid confusion with the Vault Settings of a Business Unit.
