## Tabular Views

Data in Vault can now be view from the Vault team in tabular format. To activate this, click on the table icon.

![Screenshot](/images/intercom/product-updates/12-march-2025/01-screenshot-2026-03-12-at-10-07-14.png)

You can choose which columns to include and adjust additional settings for each column.
