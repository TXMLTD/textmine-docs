## Uploading

TexMine has implemented improved bulk importing functionality which allows you to carry on with your tasks in TextMine whilst the uploads are happening.

![Screenshot](/images/intercom/product-updates/6-february-2025/01-screenshot-2025-02-07-at-12-04-04.png)

![Screenshot](/images/intercom/product-updates/6-february-2025/02-screenshot-2025-02-07-at-12-04-17.png)

![Screenshot](/images/intercom/product-updates/6-february-2025/03-screenshot-2025-02-07-at-12-04-28.png)

A preview of the uploads appears in the bottom right and allows you to carry on with any ongoing tasks within the platform.

## Downloading

Data in Legislate can now be downloaded directly via the selector.

![Screenshot](/images/intercom/product-updates/6-february-2025/04-screenshot-2025-02-07-at-12-12-41.png)

Moreover, you now have the option to download the annotated PDFs for the selected files.

![Screenshot](/images/intercom/product-updates/6-february-2025/05-screenshot-2025-02-07-at-12-13-14.png)
