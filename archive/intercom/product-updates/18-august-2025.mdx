Organisations can now track high level analytics across their business units. These analytics include the status of documents uploaded to Vault, the overall accuracy by document type and the number of pages processed by their team.

![Screenshot](/images/intercom/product-updates/18-august-2025/01-screenshot-2025-08-18-at-17-38-53.png)

The tracking period for each chart can be changed and your preference will be saved automatically based on your select.

![Screenshot](/images/intercom/product-updates/18-august-2025/02-screenshot-2025-08-18-at-17-39-35.png)

To enable analytics for a specific role, the admin of the organisation will need to enable the "View Vault analytics" permission under Vault permissions.
