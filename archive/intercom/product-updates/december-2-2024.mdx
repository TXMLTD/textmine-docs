In the original pagination release, we were not able to provide pagination for certain documents such as scans. In this release we now offer pagination for any document which is uploaded to Vault.

We have also introduced the ability for documents in Legislate to be resubmitted to Vault. This can be useful if you would like to extract new data from your documents. You can send a document back to Vault by clicking on the 3 dots in Legislate and selecting "Move document to Vault".
