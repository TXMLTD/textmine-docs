## Reasoning Tags

We now offer reasoning tags for both custom tags and default document type tags. They can also be applied to aggregation tags.

![Screenshot](/images/intercom/product-updates/17-december-2025/01-screenshot-2025-12-17-at-07-47-05.png)

When enabled, the model will provide additional reasoning to how it found the answer. You can choose to include it in the answer.

![Screenshot](/images/intercom/product-updates/17-december-2025/02-screenshot-2025-12-17-at-08-02-41.png)

![Screenshot](/images/intercom/product-updates/17-december-2025/03-screenshot-2025-12-17-at-08-02-51.png)

## Agent Results and exports

Agent results now present links to the source files and expandable cells to better interpret the results. The results table can also be exported to CSV.

![Screenshot](/images/intercom/product-updates/17-december-2025/04-screenshot-2025-12-17-at-08-06-22.png)

![Screenshot](/images/intercom/product-updates/17-december-2025/05-screenshot-2025-12-17-at-08-06-34.png)

## Improved Legislate Team view

The Legislate team view now offers configurable columns and allows you to also view when documents were published from Vault to Legislate as well as the assignee and export date.

![Screenshot](/images/intercom/product-updates/17-december-2025/06-screenshot-2025-12-17-at-07-40-24.png)

![Screenshot](/images/intercom/product-updates/17-december-2025/07-screenshot-2025-12-17-at-07-40-35.png)

## Delete personal working teams

You can now delete working (personal) teams as long as they don't have any documents in either Vault or Legislate. Business Units/teams connected to an organisation can't be deleted.

![Screenshot](/images/intercom/product-updates/17-december-2025/08-screenshot-2025-12-17-at-07-41-16.png)

![Screenshot](/images/intercom/product-updates/17-december-2025/09-screenshot-2025-12-17-at-07-41-30.png)
