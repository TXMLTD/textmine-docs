It's now possible to delete documents in Vault in bulk. Simply select the document you wish to delete and press the delete button icon.

![Screenshot](/images/intercom/product-updates/2-april-2025/01-screenshot-2025-04-02-at-08-23-40.png)

We have also introduced pagination which will accelerate the page loading times for teams with a significant amount of uploads. Pagination preferences are saved per user and team.
