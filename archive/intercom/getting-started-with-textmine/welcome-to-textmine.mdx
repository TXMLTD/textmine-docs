Welcome to TextMine, the E2E knowledge base for your business critical documents! You can use TextMine to extract data from existing documents, explore the data and create new documents from your own template library.

TextMine has 3 components:

## Vault

Vault is a module for automatically tagging your business-critical documents with AI.

![Screenshot](/images/intercom/getting-started-with-textmine/welcome-to-textmine/01-screenshot-2024-04-10-at-10-19-43.png)

## Legislate

Legislate is a module for exploring the data in your documents. Locate them one by one, answer aggregate queries and export raw data for further analysis.

![Screenshot](/images/intercom/getting-started-with-textmine/welcome-to-textmine/02-screenshot-2024-04-10-at-10-21-03.png)

## Scribe

Scribe is a module for creating smart templates which can be used by the rest of the business to configure documents by completing forms.

![Screenshot](/images/intercom/getting-started-with-textmine/welcome-to-textmine/03-screenshot-2024-04-10-at-10-24-19.png)
